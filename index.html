EPEE FENCING

Épée fencing is a modern sword-fighting sport. The whole body is a valid target. Points are scored with the tip of the weapon.

+
FOIL FENCING

Foil fencing is a fast and precise sport. Only the torso is a valid target. Touches are scored with the tip and follow right-of-way rules.

+
SABRE FENCING

Sabre fencing is a fast and aggressive sport. The target area is from the waist up. Points are scored with the edge or the tip, using right-of-way rules.

About Sportfit
We train champions and proudly represent our country in competitions.

Our fencing club teaches all three Olympic fencing styles: épée, foil, and sabre. We welcome beginners and experienced fencers of all ages. Our coaches focus on skill, discipline, and fun, helping each fencer grow and compete with confidence. Whether you want to fence for fun or train for competitions, you’ll find a supportive and professional environment with us.

Why Fencing?
Build physical fitness

Fencing improves strength, speed, balance, and endurance through fast, full-body movements.

Sharpen mental focus

Fencing trains quick thinking, concentration, and decision-making under pressure.

Boost self-confidence

Fencing builds confidence through personal progress, competition, and mastering new skills.

Learn discipline

Fencing teaches discipline through structured training, respect, and consistent practice.

Develop strategy skills

Fencing requires planning, analyzing opponents, and making smart tactical decisions.

Enjoy unique sport

Fencing combines athleticism, strategy, and history in a one-of-a-kind experience.
